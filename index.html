New York

Given the city's expansive nature, I can research and book accommodation best suited to your needs and the areas you wish to explore - Broadway, Statue of Liberty, Central Park, among others. I will arrange ticket bookings for city tours, musicals, sports events, or concerts you're interested in. With hundreds of restaurant options, I can guide you to the best spots, considering your gastronomical preferences. The target audience is quite wide here; business travelers, families, and culture enthusiasts alike, as New York is a city full of business opportunities as well as a pulsating culture & art scene.

Rome

An antique city, known for its glorious history and mouth-watering cuisine. I'll help arrange guided tours to the Colosseum, Vatican City and Patheon, among others, with professional guides fluent in your language. Also, to save time and hassle, I will book skip-the-line tickets where possible. I can suggest the best gelato places and advice on local dishes you must not miss. The primary travel audience includes history buffs and foodies.

Milan

This city is a global hub for fashion and design. I can arrange exclusive appointments at designer showrooms, get you behind-the-scenes access at Milan Fashion Week, or book tours to the spectacular Duomo. Milan is also home to world-renowned museums, like the Last Supper and La Scala Opera. I'll ensure you have reserved spots at these must-visit places. Primarily, luxury shoppers, design enthusiasts, and art lovers find Milan to be their go-to destination.

Dubai

Known for its opulence and grandeur, my service offers booking at ultra-luxury hotels or unique lodgings such as underwater hotels. I can arrange desert safaris, helicopter tours for a bird's-eye view of the city, or a visit to the world's tallest building - Burj Khalifa. I can get you reservation to dine at Pierchic, the world-renowned restaurant on stilts. Dubai attracts luxury seekers, adventure junkies, and families attracted by the variety of theme parks.
